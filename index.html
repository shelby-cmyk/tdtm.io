FAQ

Questions, answered.

Where does the data come from?
We license a national identity-graph dataset that spans hundreds of audience categories (demographics, property attributes, life events, intent signals) and apply our own filters, suppression, and AI matching on top. Same quality you'd see from any major B2C data provider. We just hide the plumbing.
How does the website pixel work?
Paste one line into your site's <head>. It resolves 25–40% of anonymous visitors to real, contactable identities, and a one-line tdtmTrack() call ties conversions (calls booked, quotes, purchases) back to those people. No plain-text personal data is stored in the browser, and it's CCPA/GDPR/TCPA-ready.
Can I push audiences to my ad accounts?
Yes. Build an audience in chat and ask us to send it to your ad account. We push encrypted identifiers — never raw records — on a schedule, then show you what converts. Streaming TV is live today, with social platforms rolling out.
What's a lookalike audience?
Upload a CSV of customers who converted. We match them to the identity graph, run a regression against a control sample, and tell you what makes them different — age, home value, tenure, income. Then you can pull more people who look like them. Included on every plan.
Is there an API?
Yes — enrich a batch of records, or resolve a single identifier (phone or hashed email) in real time, fast enough for live call flows. Try it in the API playground and pay only for the matches you get. Full reference opens up once you have a key.
Can I suppress my existing customers?
Yes — drop a CSV into the chat at any point and the bot dedupes against it before sending you the file. Phones or last-name + address + ZIP both work. We never store the raw file, only normalized signatures, with 24-hour auto-delete.
Do you support DNC compliance?
Cell DNC flags are filtered at pull time. Landlines are returned as-is. DNC obligations there vary by use case and jurisdiction, so we don't make that decision for you.
